Common Questions

Below are some of our most frequently asked questions. If you have any others, don’t hesitate to reach out to us by email.

What is Plantora?

PlantSmart is your online platform for buying and selling a wide variety of plants. We offer carefully curated options for every plant enthusiast, from beginners to experienced gardeners, all available with easy browsing and ordering.

How can I request a specific plant?

If there’s a particular plant you’re looking for that’s not listed on our site, you can easily make a request through the “Plant Wishlist” feature on our app or website. Our team will notify you if it becomes available.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes! You can download the PlantSmart app on iOS and Android. It offers the same easy ordering experience as our website, along with exclusive plant care tips and reminders for your purchased plants.

Can I use PlantSmart on other browsers?

Absolutely! Our site is fully compatible with popular browsers, including Chrome, Safari, Edge, and Firefox, so you can browse and buy from any device.
